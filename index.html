Women in Statistics (WinS)

Matched Pairs Mentorship Program Spring 2023

Matched Pairs is a mentorship program within the Stat & DS community, bringing together students from all different backgrounds. All students (undergraduate and graduate) of any gender indentity are encouraged to apply. Pairs will be assigned based on common interests, goals, and backgrounds which could include international student concerns, LGBTQ+ experiences, challenges with underrepresentation in STEM, and more.

We hope these mentor pairs last throughout the year and beyond!
